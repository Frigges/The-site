Damage Resistances: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Thunder
Condition Immunities: Charmed
Senses: Darkvision 60ft, passive perception 20
Languages: Avashic, Arkanian, Héri, Sylvan

Cunning Action: Tajir can take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as bonus action.

Evasion: When Tajir is subjected to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, he instead takes no damage if he succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if he fails.

Innate Spellcasting: Tajir's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma. He can innately cast the following spells (spell save DC 15), requiring no material components:

At will: Booming Blade, Mage Armor (self only), Prestidigitation, Thorn Whip
2/day each: Bless, Misty Step
1/day each: Faerie Fire, Invisibility

Sneak Attack: Once per turn, if any of the conditions below are true, Tajir can deal an extra 4d6 damage to a creature he hits with an attack.

  • Tajir had advantage on the attack roll.
  • Another enemy of the creature is within 5ft of it, and Tajir didn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
  • Tajir is within 5ft of ONLY that creature, and he didn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

Actions

Booming Kopesh: Spell, Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft, one creature.
Hit: 2d8 thunder damage & 1d6+7 (and sneak attack) slashing damage. If the slashing damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, Tajir gains 2d6 temporary hit points. In addition, if the target willingly moves before the start of Tajir's next turn, it takes 3d8 thunder damage.
Even if this attack misses, the target can't make oppurtunity attacks against Tajir for the rest of this turn.

Thorn Whip: Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, range 30ft, one creature
Hit: 3d6 piercing damage, and the target is pulled up to 10ft closer to Tajir.

Reactions

Uncanny Dodge: When an attacker that Tajir can see hits him with an attack, he can use his reaction to halve the attack's damage against him.
